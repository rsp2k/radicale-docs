## Why Radicale?

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="RFC Compliant" icon="approve-check">
    85-98% compliance across major CalDAV/CardDAV standards including RFC 3253 (DeltaV versioning), RFC 6638 (Scheduling), and RFC 6047 (iMIP email).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lightweight & Simple" icon="rocket">
    Easy to install and configure. Runs on any platform with Python 3.9+. Perfect for personal use or small teams.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Feature Rich" icon="star">
    CalDAV scheduling with email notifications, git-backed versioning, WebSocket real-time sync, Prometheus metrics, and LDAP authentication.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open Source" icon="open-book">
    Free and open source under GPLv3. Active development and community support.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
